SEPs in the Heliosphere

Investigating solar energetic particles (SEPs) as they propagate through the heliosphere, affecting planetary environments and spacecraft electronics.

SEPs in the Geospace

Studying how SEPs interact with Earth's magnetosphere, ionosphere, and radiation belts, impacting satellites and human spaceflight safety.

Moon-to-Mars Architecture

Assessing space weather hazards and radiation exposure for astronauts traveling beyond Earth's magnetosphere, crucial for Artemis and Mars exploration missions.
